Meet Our Highest Donator

10/19/17 | 10:25 am

Have you seen our biggest fan, Jessica Weltern? Earlier this year, Jessica asked her parents for an octopus stuffed toy, after finding about RTC. “Jessica wanted one for her birthday, for Thanksgiving, and even as an early Christmas present; she just wouldn’t stop asking for an octopus stuffy!” said Jessica’s mother. Eventually, Jessica met her BFF, “Spot,” which is a giant purple spotted octopus, ever since then, Jessica was determined to complete her collection of cephalopods! This led her to become October’s highest donator of 2017!
